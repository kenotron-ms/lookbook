Elemental magnesium bound to glycine — the most bioavailable form for neurological function. Not the cheap oxide used in most supplements. 300mg per capsule, formulated for evening use.

Why glycinate, not oxide or citrate?

Magnesium oxide is 4% bioavailable. Citrate causes loose stools at therapeutic doses. Glycinate is 80%+ bioavailable, laxative-free, and the glycine amino acid itself supports sleep onset.

Our sourcing standards

What we won't compromise on.

Third-party purity testing

Every production batch is tested by Eurofins or Covance for heavy metals, pesticide residue, microbiological contaminants, and active compound concentration before it ships. CoA available for every formula on request.

Ingredient provenance

We publish the supplier name and country of origin for every active. Albion Minerals for chelated forms. Sabinsa for botanical extracts. No contract manufacturing anonymity.

Therapeutic dosing only

We don't formulate for label claims. Every active is at its minimum clinically effective dose or above — not the trace amounts legally required to include it on the label. Bioavailable forms only.

No proprietary blends

Proprietary blends hide individual ingredient doses. We show you exactly how much of each active is in each serving, every time. If we can't list it, we don't use it.
