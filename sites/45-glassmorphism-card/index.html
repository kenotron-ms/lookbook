Why Glass

Everything a creative business needs. Nothing it doesn't.

01
Block-based portfolio canvas
Drag, resize, and compose every element of your portfolio exactly as you'd lay out a design file. No locked grids, no forced templates.
02
Custom domain in under 2 minutes
Point your DNS records at Glass and your portfolio is live at yourname.com. We handle SSL, CDN edge delivery, and uptime — you own the URL.
03
Visitor intelligence, not vanity metrics
See who viewed which projects, where they came from, and how long they stayed on each piece — without sharing your data with third parties.
04
Built-in commission and licensing tools
Take commissions, sell print licenses, and gate process content behind a paywall — all from the same dashboard as your portfolio.
05
Content protection and watermarking
Right-click protection, invisible digital watermarks, and download control per project. Your client work stays yours until you decide otherwise.
06
Full-resolution video with no compression
Stream video at native resolution with no quality loss. Motion work deserves to be seen at full fidelity, not compressed to 720p by a platform codec.
