How It Works

Built for the way real teams communicate.

Most communication tools optimize for volume. Chorus optimizes for decision quality. Every thread is structured to answer three questions: what was decided, why, and who's accountable. Your future self will thank you.

Explore the Platform

Structured Decision Threads

Every thread has a status (Open, Decided, Archived) and a designated decision-maker. Ambiguity is impossible by design.

Async-First by Default

Timezone-aware notifications. Digest modes. Focus hours. Chorus respects the cognitive cost of context-switching.

AI Context Summaries

Join any thread mid-conversation. Chorus instantly generates a decision-focused summary of everything that came before you.

Semantic Search

Search by meaning, not keyword. Find decisions, files, and context by asking plain questions in natural language.
