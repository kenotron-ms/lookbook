CURRENT THREAD — Q2 Product Strategy

Issue 001 · The Productivity Crisis

The tools multiplied.
The work got harder.

The average knowledge worker runs eleven different applications in a single day. They context-switch an estimated 300 times. Each switch costs between four and twenty-three minutes of recovered focus time. The mathematics are catastrophic.

"AURA OS doesn't ask you to change how you work. It remaps your environment to how you already think."

AURA OS is not another productivity app. It is an operating system layer that sits below your existing tools and above your file system — a unified context engine that remembers what you were doing, where you were, and why it mattered.

Read the Full Research

Core Systems

  • 01

    Context Engine

    Automatically saves your workspace state — open files, cursor position, active research, related notes — and restores it instantly. Zero friction context-switching.

  • 02

    Deep Focus Mode

    An OS-level focus mode that removes all notifications, dims irrelevant applications, and tracks your uninterrupted work streaks with millisecond precision.

  • 03

    Thought Graph

    Every note, document, search, and message is indexed into a personal knowledge graph. Ask it anything. Find the connection you forgot you'd made.

  • 04

    Canvas Layer

    An infinite spatial canvas that overlays any application. Drag content from anywhere, arrange by thought rather than folder, and export as structured documents.

  • 05

    Temporal Archive

    A complete, searchable history of everything you've worked on — not just files, but the context around them. Recreate the state of your mind from any past date.

I

Against Notification Tyranny

Every notification is a hostile takeover of your attention. AURA OS treats your focus as infrastructure — not a setting to toggle, but a system to protect at the architectural level.

II

For Spatial Thinking

Human cognition is spatial. Tab-based interfaces are not. AURA OS returns working memory to its natural form — a canvas of thought that you navigate by proximity, not alphabetical order.

III

Memory as Infrastructure

The knowledge you generate at work is yours — permanently, searchably, structurally. AURA OS treats your intellectual history as infrastructure, not ephemera.
