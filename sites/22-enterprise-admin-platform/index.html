How long does deployment take?

SENTINEL deploys in under 48 hours for cloud environments. On-premise and hybrid configurations are typically live within two weeks, including integration testing and team onboarding.

Does SENTINEL replace our existing SIEM?

SENTINEL integrates with Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, and IBM QRadar as a layer above your existing SIEM, or can replace it entirely. We provide migration support and data continuity guarantees.

Where is our data stored?

Data sovereignty is fully configurable. Choose from 18 regional data residency options across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit with customer-managed keys.

Enterprise pricing is based on protected asset volume, not seat count. No surprise costs for high-traffic events. Contact our team for a tailored proposal with a 30-day proof-of-value period.
