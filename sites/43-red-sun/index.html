How Solara Works

From concept to shipped
design without the friction.

01
Sketch your intent
Start with a rough layout, a mood, or a brief. Solara reads your design direction and extrapolates a structured system from it.
02
AI completes and extends
Solara generates responsive variants, fills component states, and builds out your token system — all respecting your design decisions, not overriding them.
03
Review with your team
Live multiplayer editing, comment threads, and approval flows built into the canvas. No third-party tool required for design review.
04
Export production-ready code
One-click React, Tailwind, or raw CSS output — aligned to your component architecture, not a generic template system.
