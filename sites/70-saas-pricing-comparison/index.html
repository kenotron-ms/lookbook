Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing cycle. No lock-in, no penalties.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Every paid plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to all features in your chosen plan during the trial period.

How does per-seat pricing work?

A seat is any user who can actively create, edit, or manage projects. Guests (view-only) don't count toward your seat count. You only pay for active seats.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is always yours. If you downgrade, features beyond your plan's limits are locked (not deleted). Upgrade again to regain full access instantly.

Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts?

Yes! Registered nonprofits get 50% off any plan. Startups in our accelerator program may qualify for extended free access. Contact us to learn more.
