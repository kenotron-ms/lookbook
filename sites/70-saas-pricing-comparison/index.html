Common questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. No cancellation penalties.

What counts as a "member"?

Any user with a full account who can create and edit tasks. Guest users (view-only) do not count toward your member limit on any plan.

Is there a non-profit or educational discount?

Yes — registered nonprofits and accredited educational institutions qualify for 40% off any paid plan. Email billing@canvas.so with documentation.

How does the Enterprise negotiation work?

We start with a 30-minute discovery call, model out your anticipated usage, and return a fixed annual quote within 5 business days. No surprise overages — ever.
