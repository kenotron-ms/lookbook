Our Process

The way we work is part of what makes it work.

Every REVEAL project follows the same four-stage process — not because we're rigid, but because we've learned what skipping any stage costs.

01

Brief & Discovery

We spend time understanding the brand, the audience, and the one thing you want viewers to feel. Strategy before aesthetics.

02

Concept & Storyboard

Two fully developed concepts, presented with rationale. Not mood boards — actual sequences with timing notes and reference footage.

03

Production & Refinement

Two rounds of revision on the approved concept. We work at 25fps from day one — no rough approximations to clean up later.

04

Delivery & Handoff

Final files in all required formats, plus a motion guidelines document your team can use for consistent future production.
