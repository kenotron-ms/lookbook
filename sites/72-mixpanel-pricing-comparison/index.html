FAQ

Common questions

How is an "event" counted?

An event is any user action you track — page views, button clicks, purchases, or custom events. Server-side events and identify calls also count. We show your usage in real-time so you're never surprised.

Can I change plans mid-month?

Yes. Upgrade instantly — you'll be charged a prorated amount for the rest of the billing period. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current period, with your data always preserved.

What happens if I exceed my event quota?

We'll alert you at 80% usage. You can choose to upgrade, purchase an overage pack, or simply have new events queued. We never silently drop data — you always know exactly where you stand.

Do you support GDPR and CCPA compliance?

Yes. INSIGHT supports user deletion, data export, consent-based tracking, IP anonymization, and EU data residency for Enterprise customers. We are fully GDPR and CCPA compliant.

How does the free trial work?

Growth plan trials run for 14 days with no credit card required. After the trial, you'll be asked to add payment info to continue — or you'll revert to the Free plan automatically with no charges.
