The Method

Precision over
probability

Standard LLMs optimize for statistical likelihood. Neural optimizes for logical validity. The difference is the gap between a confident wrong answer and a verified correct one.

1
Query Decomposition
Neural parses your question into atomic sub-queries, identifying dependencies and the optimal resolution order.
2
Parallel Source Grounding
Each sub-query is grounded against authoritative sources simultaneously. Conflicting evidence triggers arbitration before propagation.
3
Logic Chain Verification
Reasoning steps are validated against formal logic constraints before synthesis. Invalid inferences are rejected, not smoothed over.
4
Structured Delivery
The verified answer is serialized to your declared schema with citations embedded. Confidence scores accompany every field.
