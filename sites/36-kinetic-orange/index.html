How we work

The
Kinetic
method.

01
Conviction audit
We challenge your brief. Not to be difficult — because the most expensive thing you can do is build the wrong thing with confidence. We spend two hours breaking it before we commit to it.
02
Strategy sprint
Five days. One decision: what your brand stands for and who it's for. We use competitive mapping, audience forensics, and structured provocation sessions. The output is a single-page positioning doc you can actually use.
03
Visual direction
Three distinct directions presented — not three variations on one idea. Full visual system exploration across type, color, motion language, and imagery. You pick one. We go deep on it.
04
Build & ship
We produce the work. Design files, motion assets, guidelines, coded prototypes. Delivered in formats your team can actually use. We stay on call for 90 days after launch.
