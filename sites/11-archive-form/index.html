Material sourcing notes

European Oak

Air-dried for 36 months minimum. Sourced from managed forests in Austria and Slovenia. No engineered cores.

Portuguese Marble

Quarried from Estremoz and Alentejo. Each slab selected in person. Movement and veining considered part of the object's identity.

Blackened Steel

Cold-rolled mild steel, chemically patinated. Sealed with beeswax and linseed oil. No powder coating — the surface changes over decades.

Natural Linen

Undyed, unbleached Belgian linen. Pre-washed for softness. Upholstered over horsehair padding — traditional methods for durable comfort.
