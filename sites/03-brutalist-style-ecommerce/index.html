Season 04 is a refusal. A refusal of the drop-culture dopamine loop, the influencer seeding machine, and the fabric that washes out in a season. We source from three Portuguese mills. Every pattern is cut by hand and graded internally. The seams are exposed because we have nothing to hide.

This is clothing for people who find beauty in tension. In the space between a garment pulled taut and one that falls soft. In the company of silence and the discomfort of honesty.
