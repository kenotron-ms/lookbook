Core Capabilities

Four layers of ambient
intelligence, unified.

01
Perceptual Mesh

Multi-modal sensor fusion across audio, visual, biometric, and environmental streams. AURORA sees what you see, hears what matters, infers what you need — continuously.

02
Contextual Engine

A continuously updating world-model built from behavioral patterns, calendar state, social graph, and ambient telemetry. Not a snapshot — a living, evolving map of your world.

03
Intent Resolver

Translates latent intent into structured action sequences. No explicit commands. No friction. The space between thought and outcome collapses to near zero latency.

04
Sovereign Privacy

On-device inference with differential privacy guarantees. Your context model lives in your sovereign enclave — cryptographically inaccessible to AURORA infrastructure by design.
