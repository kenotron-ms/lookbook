About the Studio

Precision
in every
frame

Founded in 2014, ORBIT STUDIO is a photography practice rooted in the belief that great images begin with great listening. We work with creative directors, art editors, and brand teams across fashion, architecture, and culture.

Our approach combines technical rigor with an editorial eye trained across three continents — producing work that feels inevitable, not constructed.
