Maison Philosophy
"Darkness is not the absence of light. It is its own sovereign presence — the canvas on which all desire is painted." — Isabeau Leclair, Creative Director
I
Restraint as Luxury
True luxury is the confidence to leave space. Every seam, every cut, every absence of ornament is a deliberate statement of authority.
II
Obsidian Craftsmanship
Each piece is produced in our Paris atelier by artisans trained in traditional haute couture construction — a process that can take over 200 hours per garment.
III
Singular Materials
We source only materials with provenance: Japanese shadow-dyed silks, Scottish midnight wool, and Italian matte-finish leathers that develop character over decades of wear.
