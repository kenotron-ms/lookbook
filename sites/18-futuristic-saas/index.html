Core Features

Your co-writer.
Not your ghostwriter.

Aethon preserves your voice while removing the friction. It suggests, completes, and structures — but every word that ships is yours.

✦

Contextual Intelligence

Aethon reads your entire document before suggesting — not just the last sentence. Suggestions account for tone, argument structure, and document goals.

🎙️

Voice to Document

Dictate in any style — rough stream-of-consciousness or formal narration. Aethon structures your voice into polished, formatted prose in real time.

🧠

Style Memory

Upload 3 documents you love. Aethon extracts your stylistic fingerprint — sentence length, rhetorical devices, paragraph rhythm — and writes accordingly.

⚡

Zero-Friction Modes

Focus Mode removes all AI suggestions and tracks your unassisted words. Draft Mode accepts every suggestion inline. You control the cognitive load.

🔒

Enterprise Privacy

Zero data retention. SOC 2 Type II certified. Deployable on-premise or in your VPC. Your documents never train our models.

🔗

Deep Integrations

Notion, Google Docs, Linear, Confluence, Slack, Figma. Aethon lives in your workflow — not as a new tab to switch to, but as a layer on top.
