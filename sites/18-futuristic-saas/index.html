The workflow

From blank page to
complete draft.

01

Brief Aethon

Describe your document's purpose, audience, and tone. Aethon structures an outline before a single word is written.

02

Write with suggestions

As you type, Aethon offers completions and paragraph-level suggestions in your established voice. Accept, reject, or modify.

03

Inline refinement

Select any passage. Ask for a sharper argument, a simpler explanation, or a stronger transition. Aethon rewrites in place.

04

Final voice check

Aethon scans the complete document against your voice fingerprint. Flags anything that sounds like AI wrote it instead of you.
