Why we built this

We built SOFTLY because the apps we used made us feel watched. Every entry analyzed, every habit tracked, every mood scored. We wanted to write privately and instead we got dashboards. We wanted silence and got notifications about our silence.

SOFTLY has no metrics. Your entries are encrypted on your device. We do not read your journal. We cannot. The prompts are written by humans who have been through difficult seasons. They are not generated. They are not optimized for engagement. They are the questions that actually helped.

The ritual matters more than the result. You are not here to become a better version of yourself. You are here to understand the version that already exists. That is enough. That has always been enough.

What's Inside

Guided Reflection

Seven carefully crafted prompts each week, written to meet you where you are. Not where an algorithm thinks you should be.

Private Vault

Every word you write is encrypted locally before anything touches a server. We designed SOFTLY so that even we cannot read your entries. Your journal belongs to you, in every sense.

End-to-End Encrypted

Device-only encryption. Zero-knowledge architecture. Your key, your data.

Mood Weaving

A private emotional timeline that reveals patterns in your writing over months. No scores. No comparisons.

Weekly Letters

Each Sunday, a hand-composed letter arrives in your inbox. Reflections from past entries, offered back to you with care.
