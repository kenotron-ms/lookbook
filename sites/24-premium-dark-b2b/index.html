◆ The Process

Meticulous by design.
Every time.

01

Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough inspection using professional lighting systems and paint depth gauges. We document every imperfection and present a custom treatment plan.

02

Decontamination

Full wash, clay bar decontamination, and iron fallout removal. Your paint is chemically pure before any polishing begins.

03

Correction & Polish

Multi-stage machine polishing using premium compounds. Swirls, scratches, and marring are eliminated. Results verified under professional lighting at each stage.

04

Protection & Delivery

Coating or film application in our climate-controlled facility. Curing period, final inspection, and white-glove delivery with a documented condition report.
