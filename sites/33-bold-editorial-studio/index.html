How we work

The
Edit
Method

Four stages. No retainers padded with billable hours. You get the thinking and the making without the agency bloat in between.

01
Audit
We examine what you have, what it says, and where the signal is buried under the noise. Hard truths early save expensive corrections late.
02
Position
Strategy before style. We define what you stand for in a way that holds across every surface — not a tag-line exercise but a structural decision.
03
Build
Identity systems, digital products, campaigns, editorial direction. We make the work without farming it to contractors.
04
Hand off
Complete documentation, usage guidelines, and a system your team can operate without us. Independence is the deliverable.
