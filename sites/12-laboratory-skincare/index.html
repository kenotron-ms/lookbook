Application Protocol

Sequence matters as much
as the formula itself.

01

AM — Cleanse

Low-pH gel cleanser, 60 seconds

pH must not exceed 5.5. Alkaline cleansers disrupt the acid mantle, reducing active penetration by up to 40%. Rinse with cool water. Pat dry — do not rub.

02

AM — Water mist, 30 seconds wait

Slightly damp skin increases Niacinamide absorption

Dampened stratum corneum swells slightly, opening the intercellular lipid channels. Apply the serum at the 30-second mark — not immediately after toweling.

03

AM / PM — LAB.01-SR Serum, 3–4 drops

Press into skin. Do not rub — friction denatures active peptides

Press gently with palm to drive absorption. Begin at the centre of the face and work outward. Avoid orbital rim — retinaldehyde should not contact the eyes.

04

AM only — SPF 50+ mineral sunscreen

Non-negotiable. Retinaldehyde without SPF is counterproductive

UV exposure causes direct oxidation of retinaldehyde and azelaic acid intermediates. SPF 50+ applied as the final morning step protects your investment in the protocol.
