Our philosophy

The soil remembers
everything the farmer
does to it.

We stock only wines from certified organic, biodynamic, or otherwise verified natural farming. No irrigation without exception. No chaptalization. No commercial yeast strains. Sulphite additions, when used at all, are disclosed by bottle — not hidden in a footnote.

Farming verification

Organic or biodynamic certification, or documented farming visit by our team

Cellar transparency

Full additive disclosure. We reject wines with undisclosed SO2 at bottling.

Annual re-evaluation

Every producer is tasted and visited annually. Practices change. So do our lists.
