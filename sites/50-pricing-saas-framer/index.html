FAQ

Common questions.

Can I change plans later? +
Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you're prorated immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.
What happens at the end of the free trial? +
After 14 days, your account automatically moves to the Starter (free) plan unless you've added a payment method and chosen to continue on Pro. No charges without your explicit consent.
Is there a limit on API calls? +
Starter includes 10,000 API calls/month. Pro includes 1M/month. Enterprise is unlimited. Burst traffic is handled gracefully with automatic scaling — you'll never be hard-blocked.
Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts? +
Yes — we offer 50% off Pro for qualifying nonprofits and YC/Techstars alumni companies. Email team@orbit.app with proof of eligibility.
