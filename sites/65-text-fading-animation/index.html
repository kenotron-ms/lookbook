03 — PROCESS

How We Work

WEEK 1-2

Discovery & Strategy

We interrogate your business context, competitive landscape, and audience before touching design tools. Strategy is not decoration — it is the foundation every visual decision must survive.

WEEK 3-5

Concept & Direction

Two distinct creative directions are developed in full — not rough sketches but resolved ideas with typography, colour, motion intent, and application demonstrated.

WEEK 6-8

Refinement & Production

The selected direction is expanded across all required touchpoints. Brand guidelines are written by designers, not templated — every decision is explained and defended.

WEEK 9

Handoff & Activation

Structured asset handoff with an onboarding call for your team. We remain available for 60 days post-launch to support correct application of the brand in context.
