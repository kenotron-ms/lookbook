03 — APPROACH

Drawn by hand.
Refined by code.

PHASE 01

Concept & Proportion

Every typeface begins on paper. We study historical models, optical illusions inherent in letterforms, and the specific medium the type will inhabit before a single bezier curve is drawn.

PHASE 02

Spacing & Rhythm

Spacing is not adjusted after drawing — it is designed simultaneously. The negative space between letters is as deliberate as the strokes themselves. Kerning is tested in editorial context, not isolation.

PHASE 03

Hinting & Production

Variable font axes are engineered for smooth interpolation across the entire weight range. Screen rendering is validated at 14px, 16px, and 32px across both macOS and Windows rendering engines.

PHASE 04

Release & Support

Each family ships with a 40-page specimen PDF, implementation guidelines, and direct access to our studio for custom licensing and modification requests within 30 days of purchase.
