The Foundry Difference

Craft without
compromise

I

Optical Corrections

Every weight is individually drawn — not interpolated. Optical sizes are hand-tuned for display, text, and caption use.

II

Language Coverage

Latin Extended, Cyrillic, and Greek in every family. Over 400 glyphs per weight with full diacritic support.

III

Variable Font Technology

Continuous axes for weight and optical size. One file, infinite variations — perfectly tuned for the web.

IV

OpenType Features

Ligatures, alternates, swashes, small caps, superior/inferior figures, fractions, and ordinals built in.

V

Web Performance

WOFF2-optimized files, subset generation tools, and subsetting guidance for lightning-fast web delivery.

VI

Lifetime Updates

Your license includes all future updates. Glyphs added, bugs fixed, and new language support — always free.
