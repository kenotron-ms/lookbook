Our Philosophy

The art of
considered
hospitality

"A great hotel is not a building. It is a feeling — the sensation of being somewhere that was made entirely for you."

Since our founding in 1987, Maison Duval has operated with a single conviction: that the details are never details. Every textile, every light source, every moment of service is a conscious act of care. Our properties are not decorated — they are composed.

The Duval Experience

🍽
01
Private Dining

Each property houses a Michelin-starred restaurant open exclusively to guests. Private dining rooms available for intimate gatherings of up to twelve.

✦
02
Bespoke Concierge

Your personal Duval concierge is available around the clock — for reservations no one else can make, access no one else has, and experiences that don't appear in any guide.

◻
03
Private Spaces

Each property includes members-only drawing rooms, private terraces, and a roof garden. The spaces that matter most are never shared with the public.

☁
04
Spa & Wellness

Underground thermal spas developed in partnership with Dr. Christine Noël. Treatments draw from each city's indigenous natural pharmacopoeia.

◈
05
Art Program

Each Maison Duval property is a living gallery. Over 400 original works — curated annually. Guests may purchase any piece from the collection directly.

⊕
06
Duval Membership

Priority rates, room upgrades, annual welcome experiences, and access to our annual gathering of Maison Duval members worldwide.
